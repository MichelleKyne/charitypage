Contact

If you have any questions about Wildcat bikes don't hesitate to get in touch. We will try to accommodate you as best we can. Jane is our lead volunteer and is always happy to help any new cycling enthusiasts! You can contact her directly at jane@wildcatbikes.ie.

We are a completely volunteer based group so if you have any interest in helping please reach out! If you follow the link to www.volunteer.ie you can sign up there. We are also always keen to hear from other people who are interested in setting up their own Wildcat club!
